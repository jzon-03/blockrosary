The Five Joyful Mysteries

The First Joyful Mystery – The Annunciation, Gabriel appears to Mary

Leader:

The Angel Gabriel announces to the Virgin Mary that God wishes her to become the Mother of His Son. Mary obeys with humility and joy.

All:

I will be humble and courteous towards all.

Leader:

With the image of the Annunciation in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for us from your Son the grace of humility so that I may recite the Rosary with devotion.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Second Joyful Mystery – The Visitation, Mary visits her cousin Elizabeth

Leader:

Mary visits St. Elizabeth, who is to be the mother of St. John the Baptist. She remains and assists Elizabeth lovingly for three months.

All:

I will help my neighbor in need.

Leader:

With the image of the Visitation in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the Grace of fraternal charity so that I may always put myself out for my neighbor, loving him in God.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Third Joyful Mystery – The Nativity, Jesus is born in Bethlehem

Leader:

Jesus Christ, the Son of God is born in a stable. His Mother places Him in a manger. He is visited by the Shepherds and Wise Men.

All:

Resolution: Christ became man; I will become Christlike.

Leader:

With the image of the Nativity in mind, we pray…

All:

Mary, Queen of the Rosary, as I kneel before the Crib close to you and Joseph, obtain for me from your Son the grace that I may be poor in spirit.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Fourth Joyful Mystery – The Presentation of the Child Jesus in the Temple.

Leader:

In obedience to the law of Moses, Mary and Joseph take the infant Jesus to the Temple at Jerusalem to present Him to His Heavenly Father.

All:

I will obey the laws of God and His church as Jesus, Mary and Joseph did.

Leader:

With the image of the Presentation of Jesus in mind, we pray…

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the Grace of Chastity in thought, word and deed, so that I may avoid sins of the flesh.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Fifth Joyful Mystery – The Finding of Jesus in the Temple

Leader:

Having lost Jesus, Mary and Joseph seek Him. After three days, they find Him in the Temple talking with the learned men.

All:

I will fulfill my daily duties.

Leader:

With the image of the Finding of Jesus in mind, we pray…

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the Grace of zeal so that i may be industrious for the things of God.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…
