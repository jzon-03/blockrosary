The Five Glorious Mysteries

The First Glorious Mystery – The Resurrection

Leader:

Jesus rises from the dead (Mk. 16:6:8), glorious and immortal, on Easter Sunday. He conquers death and opens the gates of Heaven. Let our prayer meeting today raise us up and renew our lives by the Spirit that’s within us. Through baptism, our souls, dead in the tomb of original sin, radiate divine grace. Just as Christ rose and appeared to many, so should we appear to the world as followers of Christ, radiating virtue.

All:

We will live with Christ so that we may rise with Him.

Leader:

With the image of the Resurrection in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for us from your Son the grace of a renewal of Spiritual fervor, so that we may realize our richest gift is the Catholic Faith.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Second Glorious Mystery – The Ascension

Leader:

As His mother, Mary, the Apostles and disciples looked on, Jesus was lifted up and a cloud took Him out of their sight…”This Jesus, who was taken up from you into heaven, will come in the same way as you saw Him go into Heaven.” Acts 1:9,11 Our Father, make us joyful in the Ascension of your Son Jesus Christ. May we follow Him into the new creation, for His Ascension is our glory and our hope.

All:

I will follow the example of Christ on earth because I hope to be with Him in Heaven.

Leader:

With the image of the Ascension in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for us from your Son the Grace of detachment so that understanding the vanity of worldly things, we may adhere to you and heavenly realities.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Third Glorious Mystery – The Descent of the Holy Spirit

Leader:

The Holy Spirit descends upon Mary and the Apostles (Acts 1-4). Ten days after the Ascension, the Holy Spirit descends in tongues of fire upon Mary and the disciples.

All:

I will love God and my neighbor.

Leader:

With the image of the Descent of the Holy Spirit in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for us from your Son the gift of silence so that we may hear the Holy Spirit speaking in the temple that is our body.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Fourth Glorious Mystery – The Assumption

Leader:

The Assumption of Mary into Heaven (Song of Songs 2:3-6). The Apostles go to the tomb where they had laid the body of the Mother of God. They find that the angels had borne their Queen to heaven.

All:

I will praise Mary through the Rosary.

Leader:

With the image of the Assumption in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for us from your Son the grace to desire Heaven above everything in the world – power, wealth, honors.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Fifth Glorious Mystery – The Coronation

Leader:

Mary is crowned as Queen of Heaven and Earth (Lk 1:51-54). The Mother of God, to the joy of all the angels and saints, is crowned Queen of Heaven by her Divine Son.

All:

I will love Mary as my Queen and Mother.

Leader:

With the image of the Coronation in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the Grace of perseverance.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…
