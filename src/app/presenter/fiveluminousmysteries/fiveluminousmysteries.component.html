The Five Luminous Mysteries

The First Luminous Mystery – Jesus' Baptism in the Jordan

Leader:

Jesus appeared in the Jordan and insisted John the Baptist baptizes Him. As this occurs, the Holy Spirit descended upon Him in the form of a dove while the Father proclaimed, "This is my beloved Son with whom I am well pleased."

All:

As Christ was baptized I will submit also to His Father’s will.

Leader:

With the image of the Baptism of Jesus in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the grace to live the vows of my Baptism in union with Jesus my Lord.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Second Luminous Mystery – Jesus' Miracle at the Wedding in Cana

Leader:

When Christ changed water into wine, we see You, Mary, in guise of a teacher and intercessor as you urge the servants to do what Jesus commands.

All:

As Mary tells people to "Do whatever He tells you," I will do what Christ wants of me.

Leader:

With the image of the Wedding Feast in Cana in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, please intercede for me to overcome the temptations of the world, the flesh and the devil so that I may experience the joy of the Lord’s presence, power and love.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Third Luminous Mystery – Jesus’ Proclamation of the Kingdom of God

Leader:

As Jesus announced the Kingdom, He called all to conversion and forgiveness of sins, saying, "I came not to call the righteous, but sinners."

All:

I will strive to be worthy to enter Christ's kingdom through His example.

Leader:

With the image of Jesus' Proclamation of the Kingdom of God in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, please obtain for me from your Son true and perfect contrition for my sins, sincere sacramental confession in humble trust, and the Grace to amend my life for the love of God

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Fourth Luminous Mystery – The Transfiguration

Leader:

The Transfiguration shows the glory of the Godhead shining forth from the face of Christ. Peter, James and John witnessed Jesus’ face and clothes become dazzling with light, as He spoke with Moses and Elijah.

All:

As God revealed Himself to us, I will live my life through Christ always.

Leader:

With the image of the Transfiguration in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the Grace of His light here on earth so as to enjoy His glory in Heaven.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Fifth Luminous Mystery – Jesus' Institution of the Holy Eucharist

Leader:

Christ offers His Body and Blood as food under the signs of bread and wine, instituting a sacrificial memorial of His death and resurrection for the salvation of the whole world.

All:

I will recieve Christ regularly at Holy Mass and consecrate myself to Him forever.

Leader:

With the image of Jesus instituting the Holy Eucharist in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the Grace to receive him often in the sacramental and spiritual Holy Communion, always worthily and in the spirit of thanksgiving.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…
