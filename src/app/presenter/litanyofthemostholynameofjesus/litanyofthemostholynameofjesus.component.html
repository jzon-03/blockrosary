Let us pray. O Lord Jesus Christ, who hast said: ask and ye shall receive, seek and ye shall find, knock it shall be opened unto you; mercifully attend to our supplications, and grant us the gift of Thy divine charity, that we may ever love Thee with our whole heart and all our words and deeds, and may never cease from praising Thee.
Make us, O Lord, to have a perpetual fear and love of Thy holy name, for Thou never failest to help and govern those whom Thou dost bring up Thy steadfast fear and love; who livest and reignest for ever and ever. Amen
