The Five Sorrowful Mysteries

The First Sorrowful Mystery – The Agony iin the Garden

Leader:

Jesus prays in the Graden of Olives. The thought of His coming sufferings and of our sins cause Him to sweat blood.

All:

My Jesus, I will pray with all my heart.

Leader:

With the image of the Agony in the Garden in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the grace of contrition so that I may grieve over my sins which made Christ suffer.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Second Sorrowful Mystery – The Scourging at the Pillar

Leader:

our Lord jesus Christ is stripped, bound to a pillar, and cruelly scourged until His innocent Body is covered with wounds and blood.

All:

I will keep my body pure.

Leader:

With the image of the Scourging in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the Grace of patience so that I may endure all sufferings with resignation.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Third Sorrowful Mystery – The Crowning with Thorns

Leader:

A crown of thorns is pressed into the head of Jesus. His eyes are filled with tears and blood. His executioners mock and spit upon Him.

All:

My Jesus, I will banish unclean thoughts.

Leader:

With the image of the Crowning of Thorns in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the gift of mortification so that I may voluntarily suffer physical and spiritual pain for Christ the King.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Fourth Sorrowful Mystery – The Carrying of the Cross

Leader:

Weighed down by the Cross, Jesus patiently walks the road to Calvary. Mary makes the Stations of the Cross with her dying Son.

All:

My Jesus, I will bear my cross patiently.

Leader:

With the image of the Carrying of the Cross in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son a love of the cross so that I may carry it all the days of my life.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…

The Fifth Sorrowful Mystery – The Crucifixion on Calvary

Leader:

Fastened to the cross by nails, Jesus, after three hours of agony, dies in the presence of His mother.

All:

I accept the death God has prepared for me with all the pains that may accompany it.

Leader:

With the image of the Crucifixion on the Cross in mind, we pray...

All:

Our Lady of the Rosary, obtain for me from your Son the Grace of zeal to be obedient unto death even to the death of the Cross.

Sing:

Ave, Ave, Ave Maria…
