Prayer to the Holy Spirit

In the name of the Father, of the Son and of the Holy Spirit. (Use Holy Water if you have it)

Come Holy Spirit, fill the hearts of thy faithful and enkindle in them the fire of Thy Love. Send forth Thy Spirit and they shall be created. And Thou shall renew the face of the earth. Jesus, we gather in prayer today for love of You, for the conversion of sinners and in reparation for the sins committed against the Immaculate Heart of Mary.

(Repeat 3X)

Come Holy Spirit, come by means of the powerful intercession of the Immaculate Heart of Mary, your well-beloved Spouse.
