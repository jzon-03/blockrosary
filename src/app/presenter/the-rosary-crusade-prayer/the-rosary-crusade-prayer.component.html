THE ROSARY CRUSADE PRAYER

LEADER: Heavenly Father, we beg You, in the name of Jesus Christ, Your Son, to bless our Family Rosary Crusade. Give all your people and us the courage and grace to make the daily family rosary pledge and keep it faithfully.

LEADER: ALL:
Queen of the most Holy Rosary keep our families
Queen of the most Holy Rosary save our families
Queen of the most Holy Rosary bless our families
St. Therese, the Little Flower, model of holiness for us for our time pray for us
St. Lorenzo Ruiz, patron of all Filipinos abroad pray for us
The Immaculate Heart of Mary, Patroness of the Philippines and the U.S. pray for us
Lady of Guadalupe, Patroness of the Americas and the unborn pray for us
St. Francis Cabrini, Patron Saint of Immigrant pray for us
