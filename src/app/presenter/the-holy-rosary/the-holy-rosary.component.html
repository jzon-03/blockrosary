THE HOLY ROSARY

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen. (Kiss the crucifix of your rosary)
Leader:
Our Father, who art in heaven, hallowed be thy name. Thy kingdom come, thy will be done, on earth, as it is in heaven.
ALL:
Give us this day our daily bread and forgive us our trespasses as we forgive those who trespassed against us; and lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from evil. Amen
Leader:
Let us pray for the increase of faith in God, hope in God, and love of God.
Leader:
Hail Mary, Full of Grace, The Lord is with thee. Blessed art thou amongst women, and blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus. (Repeat 3X)
ALL:
Holy Mary, Mother of God, pray for us sinners now, and at the hour of our death. Amen.
Leader:
Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.
ALL:
As it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.
ALL:
O my Jesus, forgive us our sins,
save us from the fires of hell,
lead all souls into heaven, especially those
in most need of Thy mercy.
ALL Sing:
Ave, Ave, Ave Maria.
Ave, Ave, Ave Maria.
